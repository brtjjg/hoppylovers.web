# 💕 Hoppy Lovers

> **Find your people. Share what you love.**

Hoppy Lovers is a full‑featured social platform where users can connect over shared hobbies, create events, join communities, send messages, and much more. All data is stored locally in your browser's `localStorage` — no backend required to get started!

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔐 **User Authentication** – Sign up, log in, and manage your profile.
- 👤 **Profile Management** – Add a profile photo, bio, location, and up to 6 additional photos.
- 🎯 **Hobby Matching** – AI‑powered matchmaker finds your best connections based on shared interests.
- 💬 **Messaging** – Send and receive messages with other users.
- 📅 **Events** – Create and join events; see who's going.
- 🏘️ **Communities** – Join or create hobby‑based communities.
- 🔍 **Search** – Find users by username or hobby.
- 🔔 **Notifications** – Real‑time alerts for connections, messages, and more.
- 📊 **Activity Feed** – See what's happening in your network.
- 🚫 **Block & Report** – Keep your experience safe and clean.
- 🌙 **Dark / Light Theme** – Toggle between themes; your preference is saved.
- 🔗 **Share Profile** – Copy your unique profile link to share with others.
- 🛡️ **Admin Panel** – Manage users, events, communities, and reports (admin: `admin` / `admin`).
